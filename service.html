Grid Support

5$ /month

Over head/ underground grid support_

Overhead/underground grid support refers to the maintenance and repair services for the electrical grid infrastructure that supplies power to homes, businesses, and public facilities. This service can include the installation, inspection, and repair of overhead power lines, underground cables, transformers, and other components of the electrical distribution system. The goal of this service is to ensure the reliable and safe delivery of electricity to customers, while minimizing downtime and disruptions caused by equipment failure or other issues.

Delivery

15$ /month

Substation delivery_

Substation delivery refers to the process of transporting and installing electrical substations, which are key components of the power grid that convert high-voltage electricity from power plants into lower-voltage electricity that can be distributed to homes and businesses. This service can involve the delivery of pre-fabricated substations to a designated site, as well as the installation and connection of the substation components, such as transformers, switches, and control systems. Substation delivery services are essential for the expansion and maintenance of the power grid, ensuring that electricity is delivered safely and efficiently to customers.

Solar EPC

25$ /month

Solar EPC services_

Solar EPC (Engineering, Procurement, and Construction) services involve the complete design, installation, and commissioning of solar energy systems. This includes the engineering and design of the solar power plant, procurement of necessary equipment such as solar panels, inverters, and mounting structures, and the construction of the system. Additionally, Solar EPC providers may also offer project management, quality control, and maintenance services to ensure that the solar energy system is operating efficiently and effectively. These services are essential for businesses and individuals looking to transition to renewable energy sources and can help reduce dependence on traditional energy sources while also lowering energy costs and reducing carbon emissions.
